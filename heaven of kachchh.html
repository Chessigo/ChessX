Official Registration Guide

How Registration Works (Overview)

Step 1: Player must register on the ChessX platform before Tournament Registration.
Step 2: After ChessX registration, pay the ChessX membership fee of Rs. 50/-.
Step 3: Pay the Tournament Entry Fee via QR code, then register with the Google Form link.
Step 4: After successful form submission, you will receive the WhatsApp Group link.
Important: There are TWO separate payments and TWO separate registrations in this process — please do not confuse them.
1) ChessX Membership Registration (one-time, Rs. 50/-) — pay using the Gujarat Chess Club QR code.
2) Tournament Entry Registration (Google Form) — pay using the Sanjay Davda QR code.
