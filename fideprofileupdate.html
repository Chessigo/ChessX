FIDE Ratings Lookup

Look up hundreds of FIDE IDs against FIDE's own rating lists without opening profile pages one by one. Files are streamed in your browser — even large ones (100MB+) — and never leave your device.

Step 01

Get the official list

Download whichever rating list you want to look up first — Standard, Rapid, or Blitz — as a TXT file.

ratings.fide.com/download_lists.phtml →

Step 02

Choose type, then load the file

Run this once per type — pick "Rapid", load the rapid file, fetch again; then "Blitz". Standard/Rapid/Blitz ratings build up into one table. Name, Fed, Sex, Tit, WTit, OTit, FOA, K and Gms reflect whichever file you fetched most recently.

Step 03

Paste your FIDE IDs

One per line, or separated by commas/spaces. Up to 500 at once works fine.
